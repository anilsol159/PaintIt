Privacy Policy:

PaintIt respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how PaintIt handles data within the app.

Data Collection:

PaintIt does not collect or store any personal information or data from users.
The app only accesses images stored in your device's gallery when you explicitly choose to load them for editing or drawing.


Data Usage:

All drawings and edited images created within PaintIt are stored locally on your device.
PaintIt does not transmit, share, or upload any user-generated content to external servers or third parties.


Permissions:

PaintIt may request permissions to access your device's storage to save and load images. These permissions are solely used for the app's core functionality and do not involve data transmission.


AdMob Integration:

PaintIt integrates AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display ads within the app.
AdMob may collect and use non-personal information (such as device information, ad impressions, clicks, and interactions) for ad personalization and measurement purposes.
You can learn more about AdMob's data collection and usage practices by referring to Google's Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy.


Third-Party Services:

Other than AdMob, PaintIt does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks that collect user data.


Security:

PaintIt takes measures to protect your data stored on your device, including drawings and edited images. However, we recommend regular backups to safeguard your creations.
